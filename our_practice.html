Our Practice & Registration

We offer a full General Practice services and run specialist clinics for Travel vaccinations, evening clinics, childhood vaccinations, antenatal clinics, chronic diseases management clinics (diabetes, asthma, hypertension etc), NHS health checks, cervicals screening clinics and minor surgery clinics.

Please contact the local CCG for services we do not provide. The Practice is currently taking part in FY2 training and is research active.

Please complete one of our registration forms, which are available in reception. On the form, you can state which practitioner you would prefer to see. However, you will be registering with the practice rather than an individual GP and the availability of a particular GP cannot be guaranteed.

Queens Road Surgery is a foundation training practice. This means that from time to time you will be seen by a FY2 GP.

We aim to treat our patients courteously at all times and expect our patients to treat our staff in a similarly respectful way.

We take any threatening, abusive or violent behaviour, verbal or physical against any of our staff or patients seriously. If a patient is violent or abusive we may exercise our right to take action to have them removed, immediately if necessary, from our list of patients.

Patient confidentiality

We respect your right to privacy and keep all your health information confidential and secure.

It is important that the NHS keeps accurate and up-to-date records about your health and treatment so that those treating you can give you the best possible advice and care.

This information is only available to those involved in your care and you should never be asked for personal medical information by anyone not involved in your care. You have a right to know what information we hold about you.

If you would like to see your records, please contact our practice manager.
