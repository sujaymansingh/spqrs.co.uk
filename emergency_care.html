Accident and emergency/999

Accident and emergency departments at the Leicester Royal Infirmary is open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year and can assess serious injuries and provide emergency treatment.

Whatever the day or time, if you or someone else experiences significant breathing problems, severe chest pain, loss of blood, cuts, head injury or suspected broken bones, go to your nearest accident and emergency department or call 999.
